Kevin Farris | Singer | Songwriter | Chicago, IL

Kevin Farris

“I think the Midwest has shaped a lot of who and what I am”

A product of the Chicagoland area, like many, Kevin’s musical journey started way back when...with familial influence, and a desire to dive deep into his own artistry.

After several years and iterations of different touring bands and projects, his journey evolved into an international television appearance as a solo artist on NBC’s hit show, The Voice as a member of Nick Jonas’s team. The appearance provided a broader introduction into his gritty/soulful sound rife with catchy melodies and introspection.

Since then, Kevin has had the opportunity to work alongside fellow Midwest musicians Matt Walker (Smashing Pumpkins, Morrissey, Filter) and Jim Peterik (Survivor, The Ides of March) while continuing to perform and write for his latest ep.
